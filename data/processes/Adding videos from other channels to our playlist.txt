Adding Videos from Other Channels to Our Playlist

What: Adding videos from channels other than ours to the Data Engineering Zoom Cam playlist.

Why: To enrich our playlist with relevant content, ensuring a comprehensive resource for our audience.

When: Whenever a suitable video from an external channel is identified for inclusion in the playlist.

1. Click “Save”

Select the option to save the video.

2. Select Playlist

Choose the "Data Engineering Zoom Cam" playlist from the list.

3. Organize Location

Place the video in the appropriate location as per existing guidelines.
