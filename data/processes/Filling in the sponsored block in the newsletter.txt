Filling in the sponsored block in the newsletter

What: Filling in the sponsored block of the newsletter.

Why: To ensure the newsletter effectively showcases sponsored content.

When: Whenever a new sponsored item is received.

1. Download the visual

Access the image file sent for the sponsorship and download it.

2. Upload the image

Click on the image upload button and select the downloaded image from your files.

3. Add the link

Insert the link provided by the sponsor into the designated field for the button.

4. Enter the title

Paste the title provided by the sponsor into the title field.

5. Add text

Input the text associated with the sponsorship. If specific text is not available, use a placeholder like "text on attention" to indicate this.

6. Handle missing fields

If there is no visual, leave the visual field empty. If the title is unknown, leave the title field blank.
