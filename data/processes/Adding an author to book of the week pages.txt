Adding an Author to Book of the Week Pages

What: Adding an author to the book of the week pages.

Why: To ensure accurate attribution and recognition for featured books.

When: Perform this task when preparing or updating the book of the week pages.

1. Access the Editing Interface

Navigate to the section where you can edit the book of the week pages.

2. Locate the Book

Search for the specific book that requires an author to be added.

3. Add Author Details

Insert the necessary author information in the designated fields within the editing interface.

4. Commit Changes

Finalize your edits by committing the changes, ensuring that updates are saved and reflected in the system.
