Matching Payments with Invoices in Finom

What: Linking received payments to their corresponding invoices in the Finom system.

Why: To ensure accurate financial records and track payment statuses effectively.

When: Perform this task whenever a payment is received that is not automatically linked to an invoice.

1. Identify the Payment

Locate the payment received from the client (e.g., SDB) that is not linked to an invoice. 

2. Access Payment Details

Go to the specific payment entry in the Finom system.

3. Attach the Invoice

Select the option to attach a document. Choose the relevant invoice from the list (e.g., "SDB Inc.") to link it to the payment.

4. Confirm the Link

Verify that the invoice is now noted as paid and linked to the appropriate payment. Ensure all records are accurate.
