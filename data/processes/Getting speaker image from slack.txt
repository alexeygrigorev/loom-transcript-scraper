Getting Speaker Image from Slack

What: Extracting images from Slack for speaker profiles.

Why: To obtain a reliable image of speakers for creating their profiles.

When: When needing to create or update a speaker profile.

1. Access the Speaker's Profile

Navigate to Slack and use the search function (Ctrl + K) to find the speaker by name. Click on their profile.

2. Extract the Image

Right-click on the image and select "Save image as..." to download it. This ensures you have a stable copy, as the original link may not remain reliable for future access.

3. Upload the Image

Use the downloaded image to create or update the speaker profile accordingly.
