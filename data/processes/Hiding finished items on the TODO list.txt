Hiding Finished Items on the TODO List

What: Hiding completed tasks in a TODO list.

Why: To maintain a cleaner, more focused list by reducing clutter from finished items.

When: When tasks are completed but not yet ready to be permanently deleted.

1. Identify finished items

Review the TODO list to determine which items have been completed.

2. Hide completed tasks

Select the completed tasks and use the hiding feature in the TODO list application to conceal them.

3. Optional: Remove tasks later

After some time, consider permanently deleting the hidden tasks if they are no longer needed for reference.
