Pinging Prospective Speakers After They Accepted the Invite

What: Follow up with prospective speakers who have accepted an invitation but have not responded.

Why: To engage speakers and encourage them to participate after they have shown initial interest.

When: After a prospective speaker accepts an invitation and does not reply.

1. Identify the Speaker

Locate the speaker who has accepted your invitation but has not followed up with any response.

2. Prepare a Follow-up Message

Use a template or format that encourages engagement. Be polite and remind them of their acceptance to the event.

3. Send the Follow-up Message

Reach out to the speaker through their preferred communication channel, reminding them of their acceptance and encouraging them to respond.

4. Monitor Responses

Keep track of responses to your follow-up message. Be prepared to engage further if they show interest or have questions.
