Bulk Forward Multiple Telegram Messages to the Bot

What: Forward multiple messages from Telegram to a bot.

Why: To efficiently share multiple messages at once without sending each individually.

When: When there is a need to share several messages with a bot on Telegram.

1. Select Messages

Press and hold on a message to enter selection mode. Choose multiple messages by tapping on them.

2. Forward Selected Messages

Click the forward button, select the bot as the recipient, and then click send. All selected messages will be forwarded to the bot.
