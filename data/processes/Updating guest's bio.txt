Updating Guest's Bio

What: Update the biography of a guest based on their request.

Why: To ensure accurate and current information is presented about guests at events.

When: When a guest requests a modification to their biography.

1. Receive Request

Confirm the guest's request for a biography update.

2. Access Guest Data

Navigate to the appropriate data documentation system where the guest biographies are stored.

3. Locate Current Biography

Search for the guest's current biography using their name or relevant details.

4. Make Changes

Update the biography with the new information provided by the guest.

5. Save Changes

Ensure all modifications are saved accurately in the system.

6. Publish Update

Perform the action to push the updated biography to the website.
