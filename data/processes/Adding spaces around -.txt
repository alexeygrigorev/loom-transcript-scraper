Adding spaces around dashes

What: Ensure proper formatting by adding spaces around dashes in text.

Why: Proper formatting improves readability and visual consistency.

When: Apply formatting during the editing process before finalizing any text.

1. Identify dashes in the text

Check the document for instances of dashes that lack surrounding spaces.

2. Add spaces around dashes

Insert a space before and after each dash to enhance clarity and alignment. 

Ensure that other formatting issues, such as column widths, are addressed for better overall layout.
