Live Stream Attendance Stats

What: Determine and report the actual attendance of webinars.

Why: To provide accurate attendance statistics and avoid misleading information based on registrations alone.

When: When inquiries about webinar attendance are received.

1. Access Analytics

Go to the specified link for the webinar and open the analytics section to view the actual number of attendees.

2. Capture Attendance Data

Take a screenshot of the attendance stats displayed in the analytics for documentation and reporting purposes.

3. Communicate Statistics

When asked about attendance, share the actual number of attendees captured from the analytics, ensuring clarity that not all registrants may have attended.
