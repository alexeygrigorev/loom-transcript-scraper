A Few Comments About the Podcast Document

What: Creating a podcast document

Why: To organize topics and information before recording the podcast.

When: Prior to each podcast episode.

1. Identify Topics and Guests

Determine the main topics to be discussed and confirm the guest's name.

2. Replace Placeholder Text

Replace any placeholder text (e.g., "X") with relevant content, such as "recruiting lead engineers."

3. Format Titles

Use the standard title format: Subtitle - Full Name of the Guest. Ensure clarity in positioning.

4. Document Additional Details

Include any relevant notes or information that will help in producing the episode.

5. Review the Document

Check for any inconsistencies or unclear sections to ensure the document is ready for use.
