Adding Links After the Stream is Over

What: Include relevant links in the video description after an event.

Why: To provide viewers with additional resources and references related to the content.

When: After the event stream is completed.

1. Gather Links

Reach out to relevant team members to collect links they want included in the video description. 

2. Edit Video Metadata

Add the links collected to the video description.

3. Categorize the Video

Assign the video to the appropriate playlist based on its content type (e.g., webinar or podcast).

4. Update Thumbnail

If necessary, update the video thumbnail to ensure it aligns with the content.

5. Review and Publish

Check for accuracy in links and metadata, then publish the updated video.
