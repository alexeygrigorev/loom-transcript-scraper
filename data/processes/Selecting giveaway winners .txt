Selecting Giveaway Winners

What: Selection process for giveaway winners

Why: To fairly choose recipients for the giveaway items

When: After the giveaway duration ends

1. Prepare for Winner Selection

Copy the relevant entries from the promotional source.

2. Access the Winner Selection Tool

Navigate to the designated tool for selecting winners and select the appropriate options based on the entries.

3. Execute the Winner Selection

Run the selection workflow to identify the winners.

4. Review Selected Winners

Check the output for winners in the designated folder.

5. Decide on Messaging Content

Draft the message to notify the winners, using a template if available.

6. Notify Winners

Send out the selection notification from the designated account, or coordinate with the account holder for sending messages.
