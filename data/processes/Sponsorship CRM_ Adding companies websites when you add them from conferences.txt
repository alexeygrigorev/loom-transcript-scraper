Sponsorship CRM: Adding Companies' Websites When Adding Them from Conferences

What: Add website links for companies that are recorded from conferences in the CRM.

Why: Including website links facilitates easier access to company information for future reference and content preparation.

When: Whenever a company is added to the CRM from a conference.

1. Identify the company

Locate the company name from the conference list, e.g., Microsoft Azure.

2. Access the company link

Click on the company's profile to retrieve the corresponding website link.

3. Input the website link

Paste the website link into the designated field in the CRM associated with the company's entry.

4. Verify the link

Ensure that the link is correct and functional to facilitate future content additions.
