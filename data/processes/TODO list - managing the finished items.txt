Managing Finished Items in a To-Do List

What: Organizing completed tasks in a to-do list.

Why: To enhance clarity by separating completed tasks from pending ones, reducing confusion and streamlining script management.

When: Perform this process after completing a task or at the end of a work session.

1. Move Completed Tasks

Drag and drop completed items to the designated section for finished tasks.

2. Organize Remaining Tasks

Ensure all incomplete items remain in the active task area for easy access.
