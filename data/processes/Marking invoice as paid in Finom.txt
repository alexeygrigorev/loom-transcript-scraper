Marking Invoice as Paid in Finom

What: Linking payment to an overdue invoice in Finom.

Why: To ensure proper financial tracking and management of outstanding invoices.

When: When an invoice is overdue and payment has been received but not automatically linked.

1. Access the Invoice

Navigate to the invoice that is marked as overdue. 

2. Initiate Payment Link

Click on the payment option linked to the invoice. 

3. Confirm Payment Association

Select the corresponding payment entry to associate it with the invoice.

4. Finalize the Link

Ensure the payment is successfully linked. If there are issues, repeat the process until confirmation is achieved. 

5. Verification 

Return to the invoice to verify that the payment has been marked accordingly.
