Processing Transcripts and Timecodes

What: Management of video transcripts and timecodes.

Why: To ensure proper organization and accessibility of video content and corresponding transcripts for effective processing and public availability.

When: After uploading video content and when preparing transcripts for publication.

1. Upload video content

Upload the video to the designated platform.

2. Organize timecodes

After upload, timecodes should be processed and placed in the timecodes folder.

3. Access transcripts

Transcripts are stored in a separate transcripts folder for clarity and easy access.

4. Prepare for publication

When the transcripts are ready, prepare them for addition to the website alongside the corresponding video.

5. Monitor updates

Ensure that updated transcripts are processed when needed and placed in their designated location for future use.
