Converting USD to EUR for Revolut Fees

What: Convert US dollars to Euros for bookkeeping purposes using current exchange rates.

Why: To accurately record foreign currency transactions and associated fees in our financial records.

When: Perform the conversion whenever USD is exchanged for EUR, particularly when using Revolut.

1. Determine the exchange rate

Access the current exchange rate for converting USD to EUR.

2. Calculate the total amount in Euros

Multiply the amount of US dollars by the exchange rate to find the equivalent amount in Euros.

3. Record the amount in the bookkeeping spreadsheet

Input the calculated Euro amount into the bookkeeping spreadsheet for accurate financial tracking. 

4. Store relevant documentation

Add any associated fees and conversion documentation as a PDF to Dropbox for future reference.
