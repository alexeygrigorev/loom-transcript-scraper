Luma - Removing Email Notifications

What: Disable email notifications for new sign-ups in Luma.

Why: To reduce unnecessary email noise from multiple sign-up notifications.

When: When creating events in Luma.

1. Access Luma settings.

Locate the section that manages email notification preferences.

2. Disable sign-up email notifications.

Tick the option to turn off email alerts for new sign-ups. This prevents receiving further notification emails. 

3. Update process documentation.

Incorporate this change into the process for creating events to ensure the team is aware of the updated workflow.
