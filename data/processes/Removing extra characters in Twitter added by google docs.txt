Removing Extra Characters in Twitter Added by Google Docs

What: Remove unwanted spaces and extra characters from Twitter text generated through Google Docs.

Why: To ensure that the text adheres to character limits and avoids formatting issues when shared on Twitter.

When: Whenever text is prepared for posting on Twitter and has been edited or formatted in Google Docs.

1. Check the text for spaces

Inspect the content to identify any irregular spaces or formatting issues introduced by Google Docs.

2. Edit the content

Remove any unnecessary spaces or extra characters to ensure the text meets Twitter's character limits.
