Sharing Dropbox Links with Pavel

What: Sharing Dropbox links to podcast tracks in addition to YouTube links.

Why: To provide easy access to necessary audio files alongside video content.

When: When sharing content with Pavel that includes both podcast audio and YouTube video links.

1. Locate the podcast folder

Navigate to the designated folder containing the podcast tracks.

2. Get the link for each track

Select the desired audio track, click on the share option, and copy the link.

3. Compile links

Combine the Dropbox links to the podcast tracks with the corresponding YouTube video links.

4. Share the compiled links

Send the complete list of links to Pavel for access.
