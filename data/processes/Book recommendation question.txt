Book Recommendation Process

What: Adding a book recommendation question to podcast documents

Why: To gather resource recommendations for listeners and streamline podcast preparation

When: Whenever preparing or updating podcast documents

1. Review Podcast Documents

Go through all existing podcast documents and identify those in the pipeline for updates.

2. Add Book Recommendation Line

Insert the line “Are there any books or other resources that you can recommend to the listeners?” into each document.

3. Handle Canceled Guests

If a guest cancels, move their entry to a designated section for canceled episodes.

4. Update Existing Templates

Consider creating a standardized template with common questions for future podcasts and update existing documents with these.

5. Update Document Names

Ensure all documents are accurately named and reflect current information about scheduled podcasts.
