Getting invoices from Mailchimp

What: Retrieve invoices from Mailchimp account

Why: To maintain accurate billing records and track expenses

When: Whenever an invoice is required for accounting or expense management

1. Access Mailchimp

Log in to your Mailchimp account and navigate to the "Account" section, then select "Billing."

2. View Billing History

Locate the "Billing History" option to see the list of invoices.

3. Print or Save Invoice

Choose the specific invoice you need. Click "Print" to print it directly or use the "Save" option to download it.

4. Organize Invoices

Send the printed or saved invoice to the designated folder or email for records. Fill in details in the relevant spreadsheet for tracking expenses.
