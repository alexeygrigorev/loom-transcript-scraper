Asking guests to share the video with their network

What: Encouraging guests to promote the video through their social networks.

Why: To increase video visibility and reach a wider audience through the guests' networks.

When: After recording the video and before its publication.

1. Reach out to the guest

Contact the guest and express appreciation for their participation in the video.

2. Request sharing

Ask the guest if they can share the video link with their network to boost visibility.

3. Provide sharing details

Give the guest clear instructions on where and how to share the video (e.g., social media platforms, emails, etc.).
