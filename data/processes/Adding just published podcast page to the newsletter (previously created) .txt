Adding Just Published Podcast Page to the Newsletter

What: Add a newly published podcast page to the newsletter.

Why: To keep subscribers informed about the latest content and encourage engagement.

When: After the podcast page has been published on the website.

1. Access MailChimp

Log in to the MailChimp account and navigate to the newsletter draft.

2. Edit Newsletter Design

View the draft design and determine where to add the links to the newly published podcast episode.

3. Add Podcast Links

Insert the links to the new podcast page within the newsletter content to ensure it is accessible to subscribers.

4. Review Changes

Check the newsletter for any additional elements or corrections needed. 

5. Save and Schedule

Once satisfied with the newsletter design and content, save the changes and schedule the newsletter for distribution.
