Locating the Campaign

What: Identify the date of a specific complaint related to the campaign.

Why: To ensure proper tracking and follow-up on relevant complaints.

When: Whenever you need to check on a specific complaint related to the campaign.

1. Access the relevant documents.

Navigate to the documents folder where campaign details are stored.

2. Locate the specific campaign column.

Find the column labeled "newsletter sponsorship."

3. Identify the relevant date.

Look for the entry corresponding to the complaint in question, noting the date listed (e.g., 9th of May for Amani). 

4. Review the complaint.

Use the date to track down and examine the specific complaint you need.
