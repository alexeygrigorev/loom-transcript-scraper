Updating the Schedule Table When Rescheduling Events

What: Updating the schedule in the ScaleIO spreadsheet for rescheduling events.

Why: Ensure that the timeline for events is current and accurate to facilitate planning and coordination.

When: When a request to reschedule an event is received.

1. Receive Request

Check the email or communication platform for rescheduling requests.

2. Access the Schedule

Open the ScaleIO spreadsheet to review the current schedule.

3. Update Event Date

Locate the specific event that needs to be rescheduled and update the date accordingly.

4. Communicate Changes

Send updated invites and notifications to all involved parties regarding the new date.

5. Confirm Updates

Follow up to ensure all parties acknowledge the changes and have the correct information.
