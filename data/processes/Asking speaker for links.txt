Asking Speaker for Links

What: Requesting links from speakers that were mentioned during a presentation.

Why: To compile relevant resources such as blog posts or tools mentioned by speakers for distribution to attendees post-event.

When: After a webinar or event.

1. Identify Required Links

Determine which links were referenced during the presentation that should be included for attendees.

2. Contact the Speaker

Send an email to the speaker requesting any blog posts, tools, or resources shared during their presentation.

3. Update the Template

Incorporate a section in the event follow-up template to ensure all necessary links are requested in future communications.
