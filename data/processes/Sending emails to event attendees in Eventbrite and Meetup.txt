Sending emails to event attendees in Eventbrite and Meetup

What: Sending emails to attendees of events created in Eventbrite and Meetup.

Why: To communicate important information regarding the event and ensure attendee engagement.

When: Send emails typically one day before an event or as needed to provide updates and reminders.

1. Access Event Management

Navigate to the event management section of either Eventbrite or Meetup.

2. Select Attendees

Locate the list of attendees registered for the event.

3. Compose Email

Draft your email message, ensuring it contains relevant information for the attendees.

4. Send Email

Dispatch the email to the attendees, confirming that it has been sent successfully.

5. Copy Email Content 

If needed, copy the text of the email for use in another platform or for other event communications.
