LinkedIn Sales Navigator Invoices

What: Retrieve invoices from LinkedIn Sales Navigator.

Why: To maintain financial records or for reimbursement processes.

When: When invoices are needed for accounting or expense reporting.

1. Access LinkedIn Account

Log in to your LinkedIn account.

2. Navigate to Sales Navigator

Click on the Sales Navigator icon.

3. Open Settings

Select the 'Settings' option from the menu.

4. Find Billing Information

Look for the 'Billing Information' section and click on 'Billing History.'

5. Select Invoice

Locate the desired invoice, for example, for February, and click on 'Print.'

6. Save Invoice

Save the printed invoice to your device.

7. Share Invoice

Send the saved invoice to the appropriate recipient for further processing.
