Sending reports to accountants for bookkeeping

What: Sending tax support reports to accountants via secure email.

Why: To provide accountants with necessary documentation for accurate bookkeeping.

When: Send reports on a regular basis, typically monthly.

1. Create a secure email

Use the designated email address provided in your company communications.

2. Set the email subject

Format the subject line as "Data Talks Club - [Month, Year]". For example, "Data Talks Club - August 2022".

3. Prepare the report

Select the required data from the spreadsheet, specifically from columns A to F.

4. Archive the report

Follow the established protocol for archiving, ensuring to name the file appropriately: "Data Talks Club - [Year] - [Month]".

5. Send the email

Attach the report and any necessary documents, then send to the accountants.
