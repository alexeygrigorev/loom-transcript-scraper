Cleaning the Leads Spreadsheet

What: Remove outdated or declined leads from the spreadsheet.

Why: To maintain an organized and efficient lead database, ensuring that only relevant leads are tracked.

When: When the leads spreadsheet becomes cluttered with inactive or unresponsive leads.

1. Identify Leads to Remove

Review the leads in the spreadsheet and identify those that are declined, closed, or no longer relevant.

2. Delete or Hide Leads

Select the identified leads for removal. You can choose to delete them permanently or hide them temporarily based on your needs.
