Book of the Week: Changing the Status to Confirmed

What: Update the status of an event from "proposed" to "confirmed" in the announcement process.

Why: To ensure accurate tracking and communication of event status.

When: After receiving confirmation from the relevant party regarding the event date.

1. Confirm the Date

Verify that the date works for all necessary participants.

2. Fill Out the Form

Complete the required form to initiate the announcement process.

3. Make an Announcement

Create and post an announcement regarding the confirmed event status.
