Moving Podcast Documents to Archive in Google Drive

What: Move completed podcast documents to an archive folder in Google Drive.

Why: To keep the active folder uncluttered and organized by separating past podcast episodes from upcoming projects.

When: After all tasks related to the completed podcasts are finalized, typically on Fridays.

1. Identify Completed Podcasts

Locate the folder containing completed podcast episodes in Google Drive.

2. Create Archive Folder (if not already existing)

Ensure there is a dedicated archive folder for storing past podcasts.

3. Move Documents

Transfer the completed podcast documents from the active folder to the archive folder.

4. Confirm Transfer

Check that all documents have been successfully moved and are accessible in the archive folder.
