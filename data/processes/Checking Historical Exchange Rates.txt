Checking Historical Exchange Rates

What: Check historical exchange rates to verify past transaction values.

Why: To ensure accurate financial reporting and to reconcile discrepancies in payments.

When: Perform this process anytime a historical payment needs verification or clarification.

1. Gather Documentation

Locate relevant documents that show the amount paid and any related exchange rate details.

2. Identify the Historical Exchange Rate

Check the historical exchange rate for the specific date of the transaction to determine the accurate amount in the desired currency.

3. Compare Values

Analyze the obtained historical exchange rate against the original payment to identify any discrepancies.

4. Report Findings

Communicate the findings to the accounting team, detailing the amount paid and the correct historical exchange rates used for verification.
