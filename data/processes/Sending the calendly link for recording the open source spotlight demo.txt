Sending the calendly link for recording the open source spotlight demo

What: Sending a Calendly link to schedule a demo.

Why: To facilitate the scheduling of a demo recording for participants interested in the open source spotlight.

When: When someone expresses interest in doing a demo.

1. Respond to interest

Acknowledge the individual's request to do a demo.

2. Share Calendly link

Send your Calendly link to the individual, allowing them to choose a convenient time for the demo recording.
