Inviting People to Slack

What: Inviting new members to a Slack workspace.

Why: To ensure team members and contributors have access to relevant communication channels.

When: When a new member needs to be added to the Slack workspace.

1. Access the Slack invitation feature.

Navigate to the option to invite people.

2. Enter the email address.

Input the email address of the person you wish to invite.

3. Send the invitation.

Complete the process to send the invitation to the provided email.

4. Notify the invited person.

Send a brief message to inform them to check their email for the Slack invitation.
