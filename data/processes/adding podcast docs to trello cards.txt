Adding Podcast Docs to Trello Cards

What: Adding relevant documents for podcasts to Trello cards.

Why: To organize and track podcast episodes effectively within the Trello project management system.

When: After finalizing the podcast recording and preparing the necessary documents.

1. Gather podcast details

Collect the essential information about the podcast episode, including the host, guest, and episode title.

2. Create or update the Trello card

Locate the relevant Trello card for the podcast episode or create a new one if it doesn't already exist.

3. Attach documents

Upload any necessary documents related to the podcast episode, such as scripts, show notes, or promotional materials, to the Trello card.

4. Review and finalize

Check that all information is correctly entered and that documents are properly attached before concluding the process.
