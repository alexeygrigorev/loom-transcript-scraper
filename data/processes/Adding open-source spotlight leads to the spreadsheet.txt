Adding Open-Source Spotlight Leads to the Spreadsheet

What: Adding new open-source qualified leads to a shared spreadsheet.

Why: To track potential candidates for outreach related to open-source initiatives.

When: Whenever new open-source qualified leads are identified.

1. Identify the Lead

Research and gather relevant information about the new lead, including their name, LinkedIn profile, and email address.

2. Open the Spreadsheet

Access the designated spreadsheet used for tracking open-source leads.

3. Add Lead Information

Input the following details into the spreadsheet:
- Name
- LinkedIn link (if available)
- Email address

4. Save Changes

Ensure all information is saved and that the spreadsheet is updated with the new lead.
