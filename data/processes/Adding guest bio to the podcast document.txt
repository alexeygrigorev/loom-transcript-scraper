Adding Guest Bio to the Podcast Document

What: Add guest biographies to podcast documents for efficient speaker introductions.

Why: Including guest bios aids in preparing for podcast events and ensures a smooth introduction of speakers.

When: Add guest bios when preparing the podcast document.

1. Receive the guest bio.

Ensure the bio includes relevant information about the guest's background and expertise.

2. Open the podcast document.

Locate the section for guest introductions.

3. Insert the guest bio.

Place the bio in the designated area of the document for easy reference during the podcast event. 

4. Review the document.

Verify that the bio is accurate and formatted correctly before finalizing the podcast document.
