Book of the Week - Sending Winners' Emails

What: Sending congratulatory emails to authors and publishers

Why: To acknowledge winners and facilitate communication between authors and their associated publishers.

When: After the completion of the selection process for the winners.

1. Collect recipient information

Gather the necessary email addresses of authors and publishers.

2. Compose the email

Create a template for the congratulatory email. Include necessary details such as the winner's name, achievement, and any relevant information.

3. Customize the email

Insert personalized details for each recipient. Ensure that the tone is professional and friendly.

4. Send the email

Email the customized messages to each author and publisher. Make sure to include each recipient in the "To" or "CC" fields appropriately.

5. Follow-up if necessary

Check for any responses or confirmations and follow up if needed to ensure communication is complete.
