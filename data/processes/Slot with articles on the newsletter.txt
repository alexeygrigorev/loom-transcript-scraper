Slot with articles on the newsletter

What: Updating the article section in the newsletter

Why: To ensure only new articles are included, maintaining relevance and freshness.

When: After reviewing the current content before sending the newsletter.

1. Review existing articles

Check if there are any new articles since the last newsletter.

2. Verify previous content

Cross-reference with the previous newsletter edition to identify previously included articles.

3. Delete old articles

Remove any articles that are not new or have already been featured.

4. Insert new articles

If available, add the latest articles to the newsletter. If not, leave the section empty.
