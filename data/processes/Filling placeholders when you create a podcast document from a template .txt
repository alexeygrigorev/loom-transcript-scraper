Filling Placeholders When Creating a Podcast Document from a Template

What: Replacing placeholders in podcast templates with specific information.

Why: Ensures clarity and relevance in the podcast document by customizing it for each episode.

When: Whenever you create a new podcast document from a template.

1. Copy the Template

Make a duplicate of the podcast template to maintain the original formatting.

2. Replace Placeholders

Identify and replace all placeholders with the appropriate information, such as the episode name and producer details. 

3. Keep Unchanged Elements

Do not replace fixed elements, like the topic agreed upon, to maintain the integrity of the document.

4. Review Template

After making updates, review the document to ensure all necessary changes have been made and that the structure is clear.
