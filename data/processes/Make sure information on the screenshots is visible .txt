Make sure information on the screenshots is visible

What: Ensure screenshots are clear and legible.

Why: To facilitate understanding of the content by providing easily viewable images.

When: When capturing and including screenshots in documents.

1. Zoom In

Double-click on the screenshot to zoom in for better visibility.

2. Take Clear Screenshots

Capture focused and high-resolution screenshots, ensuring they are not too small or blurred.

3. Edit for Clarity

If necessary, crop or adjust the screenshots so that they are directly relevant to the content and easily interpretable.

4. Review Visibility

Before finalizing the document, verify that all screenshots are clearly visible and provide good context.
