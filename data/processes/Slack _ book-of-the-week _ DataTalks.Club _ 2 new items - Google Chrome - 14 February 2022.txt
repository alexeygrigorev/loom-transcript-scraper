Slack Book of the Week Announcement Process

What: Announce the Book of the Week on Slack

Why: To inform community members about the selected book, encouraging engagement and discussions.

When: Whenever a new Book of the Week is selected.

1. Prepare Announcement

Review the book details and the announcement format to ensure clarity and conciseness.

2. Post Announcement

Publish the announcement on the designated Slack channel. Utilize the share message feature by hovering over the message to promote it as a channel announcement.

3. Engage with the Community

Encourage community members to ask questions and share their thoughts after the announcement is made.

4. Remove Preview

If applicable, remove any pre-announcement previews from the conversation to maintain a clean chat history.
