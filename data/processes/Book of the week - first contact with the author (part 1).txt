Book of the Week - First Contact with the Author (Part 1)

What: Arrange an event featuring the author of a selected book.

Why: To promote the book and engage readers by facilitating direct interaction with the author.

When: Initiate the process upon selecting a book and confirming the author's participation.

1. Establish contact with the author

Reach out to the author through the publisher's representative. Introduce yourself and confirm the author's interest in participating in the Book of the Week event.

2. Provide event details

Send the author a link to the event page that contains comprehensive details about the Book of the Week initiative. Use the link DTC-BOTW for reference.

3. Propose a date for the event

Suggest a date for the event that aligns with the author's availability and the planned schedule. Confirm the date following the author's response.
