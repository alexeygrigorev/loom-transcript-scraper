Adding Links to Images in Mailchimp (for the newsletter)

What: Linking images in Mailchimp emails.

Why: To enhance engagement by making images clickable.

When: Whenever images are added to newsletters.

1. Select the image

Click on the image you want to link.

2. Add the link

Click on the 'Link' option and enter the desired URL. 

3. Repeat for additional images

Link both the banner and any other images, such as buttons, following the same steps. 

By ensuring all images in the email are linked, you improve the functionality and effectiveness of the newsletter.
